# Project Brief — Future Vision XPRIZE Submission

**Working title:** Project Metanoia
**Owners:** Dirk Standaert & Nejc Sušec
**Submission deadline:** 14 August 2026

## Purpose

This project aims to create a three-minute cinematic vision of a hopeful future for humanity as part of the Future Vision XPRIZE competition. Our ambition is not simply to produce a visually impressive AI-generated film, but to tell a story that leaves viewers with a renewed sense of optimism about humanity's future. Every creative and technical decision should contribute to that objective.

The competition provides an opportunity to experiment with a new creative workflow in which humans and artificial intelligence collaborate throughout the creative process. Rather than using AI solely as a production tool, we will explore how AI can actively contribute to worldbuilding, storytelling, visual design and creative iteration, while all final creative decisions remain under human direction.

## Vision

The future is often portrayed as dystopian, technologically overwhelming or environmentally catastrophic. While these narratives are compelling, they have become the dominant lens through which audiences imagine tomorrow. Our film will present a different perspective.

We want to depict a civilization that has successfully addressed many of today's structural challenges — not by creating a perfect world, but by demonstrating how technological progress, institutional innovation and human cooperation can produce societies that are simultaneously more prosperous, more resilient and more meaningful. The film should leave audiences with a feeling that such a future is not only desirable, but genuinely attainable.

## Creative Foundation

The project builds upon the existing Disordine universe and, more specifically, the Metanoia chapter. The Disordine universe already provides a mature creative foundation, including visual language, worldbuilding principles, recurring themes, character designs and stylistic guidelines. Reusing these assets allows the team to focus on developing the strongest possible narrative rather than recreating an entirely new fictional universe within a four-week production window.

The competition submission should remain accessible to viewers who have no prior knowledge of the Disordine universe. Existing lore serves as a creative foundation rather than a prerequisite for understanding the film.

## Objectives

1. **Create an exceptional competition submission** — Deliver a film that fully satisfies the Future Vision XPRIZE requirements while presenting a memorable, emotionally engaging and visually distinctive vision of humanity's future.
2. **Develop an AI-assisted creative workflow** — Design and validate a production process in which AI contributes meaningfully throughout the creative lifecycle, from early ideation to final production, while preserving human creative direction.

## Guiding Principles

- **Story before spectacle** — Beautiful visuals alone are insufficient. Every scene should serve the narrative and reinforce the emotional journey of the audience.
- **Optimism without naivete** — The future should feel hopeful because humanity has solved difficult problems — not because those problems never existed.
- **Plausibility over fantasy** — The film should remain grounded in developments that audiences can reasonably imagine emerging over the coming decades.
- **Human-centered technology** — Technology exists to expand human potential, relationships and creativity rather than becoming the central character itself.
- **Emotional resonance** — The audience should remember how the film made them feel long after they have forgotten specific visual details.
- **Simplicity** — Given the short runtime, every scene, concept and visual element must justify its inclusion.

## Scope

The project encompasses the complete creation of the competition submission, including concept development, worldbuilding, screenplay, visual development, production and delivery. The film should offer a carefully selected glimpse into a larger world that feels coherent, believable and worth exploring further.

## Deliverables

All materials required for the Future Vision XPRIZE submission, including the three-minute film and accompanying treatment. Supporting internal documentation, creative assets and production materials that enable future iterations and continued development after the competition.

## Success Criteria

- Delivers a complete competition submission before the deadline.
- Presents a coherent, inspiring and emotionally engaging vision of the future.
- Demonstrates a productive collaboration between human creators and AI systems.
